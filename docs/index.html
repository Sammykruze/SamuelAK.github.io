About Me

I'm a content designer with 3 years of experience crafting digital experiences that are both meaningful and measurable. My approach combines strategic thinking with creative execution to solve complex communication challenges.

I specialize in translating business goals and user needs into clear, compelling content that drives engagement and action. My work spans UX writing, content strategy, and information architecture across fintech, healthcare, and e-commerce.

I design visually driven content and write sharp, actionable analyses to demystify trends and emerging tech. Every video and article is tailored to bridge the gap between expert insights and audience understanding, from storyboarding to final edits.
